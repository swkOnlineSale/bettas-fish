A Legacy Bred in Sarawak

While the Betta splendens originated in the rice paddies of Thailand, the masters of Kuching have spent decades perfecting the high-contrast variants that define the modern "Obsidian" aesthetic. Our hatchery leverages the unique water chemistry of the Sarawak river basin to develop pigments that are deeper, richer, and more resilient than those found anywhere else in the world.
